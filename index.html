Becoming McGuff

An Adoption Story

Matthew and I met in October of 2007 and married in March of 2008. So yeah it was a whirlwind. As fast as we fell in love and married we spent a lot of our courtship talking about our future children. We knew that we wanted to be parents. We would go over different scenarios of how we would handle this issue or that issue in parenthood. Looking back it make me laugh, but I also see how the Lord was preparing us for what was to come.

We had Aubrey our first before our 1st anniversery. We were made to be parents together and I would not want it to be with anyone else!
